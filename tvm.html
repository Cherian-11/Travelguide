About Trivandrum

Thiruvananthapuram is Kerala's capital and one of the cleanest and greenest cities in India. The city is home to several prestigious institutions such as ISRO, VSSC, Technopark (India's first IT park), premier educational institutes like IISER, CET, and medical research centers...

History

  • Ancient Era: Known as Ananthapuri, with origins tied to Padmanabhaswamy Temple.
  • 1729: Travancore Kingdom established by Marthanda Varma.
  • 1800s: Became Travancore capital; reforms and English education.
  • 1949: Integrated into Indian Union.
  • 1956: Declared capital of Kerala.
  • Today: Hub of IT, ISRO, tourism, and culture.
